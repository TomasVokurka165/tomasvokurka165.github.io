Stocks and Trading

Authors github: Github | Authors linkedIn: LinkedIn

Algorithmic and high frequency trading

  1. Computers can execute thousands of trades per second, far faster than any human
  2. Algorithms analyze market data, spot patterns, and place trades automatically when certain conditions are met which can be beneficial for the creator or organisation incorporating said algorithm
  3. Large investment firms use AI-driven trading bots to predict short-term price movements and maximize profit margins.

Online trading platforms

  1. Websites and apps like robinhood and coinbase make it possible for anyone anywhere to trade and own stocks of there own without having to go through a broker
  2. Computers handle order matching, real-time quotes, and transaction confirmations instantly.
